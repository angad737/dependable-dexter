About Us

We aim for sophistication, and don't stop until its simple. We strive for innovation, happy users are the ripple. Enable communication in community, alone our struggles weigh a quintle. Our tech solution, work together, make our difficulties dwindle.

We develop innovative and intuitive technological solutions to some of the biggest challenges our society faces. We socially engineer our process to motivate a massive community of users to find a solution. Breadwinner takes hiring to the next evolutionary level. India's "Big Billion" is made up of fragments of "Many Millions". To bring a sophisticated technological solution to the problem of Unorganized Labor, which amounts to 94% of the of the total labor market as on 2015, our solution must engage, empower and encourage people from all stratas to participate.

Breadwinner allows employers to effortlessy swipe to browse, shortlist and schedule interviews to hire helpers in their homes or offices. It engages and engourages tech saavy users to earn by uploading profiles of helpers seeking jobs, helpers who ordinarily dont have access to or are unable to upload great job profiles

Hire Helpers

Search or Swipe through thousands of Helpers in a variety of jobs. Watch a 60 second interview of the helpers you are interested in. Check their verification and credentials. Know them well before you see them. Schedule an interview to give them a chance to work in your home or office.

Recruit & Earn

Earn a lot or a little on the side, on your own time, whenever you choose. Get paid to find helpers for your neighbors, friends, colleagues and community. It takes 5 minuets for you to ask a few questions, shoot a video and press a button; for them to get a job without you, it could take them 5 months.

Trained and Certified

Browse helpers from trusted professional staffing agencies. No need to risk being scammed or worse, all profiles are throughly examined so that you get only the most reliable and trustworthy.
